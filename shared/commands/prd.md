Based on our conversation so far, generate a Product Requirements Document.
Save it to docs/PRD.md.

Structure:
1. Problem Statement — what pain are we solving and for whom
2. Users — who uses this, what is their context
3. Goals — what success looks like, with measurable criteria
4. Non-Goals — what is explicitly out of scope for v1
5. Core User Flows — step by step, from user perspective
6. Constraints — technical, regulatory, or time constraints
7. Open Questions — anything that needs a decision before building

After writing, summarise the top 3 risks that could derail this project.

Be specific. Avoid generic statements. If something is unclear, ask before writing.
